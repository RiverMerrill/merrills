AS EASY AS MAKING A CUP OF TEA!

Now it's easy to give your houseplants the benefits of compost tea every time you water. Just drop one compost tea bag into your watering can and let it "steep" for 10 - 15 minutes. Then simply water your plants as usual. This 100% organic, nutritionally rich tea is a natural fertilizer that provides healthy microorganisms and nutrients to rejuvenate soil, wake up tired plants and promote vigorous growth. Each box of 12 compost tea bags makes 12 gallons.
